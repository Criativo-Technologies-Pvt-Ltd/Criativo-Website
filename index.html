About us

Criativo – The Future of Education

Criativo is redefining education through technology-driven intelligence, seamless automation, and student-centric innovation, built on decades of expertise in running successful educational institutions.

With 40+ years of experience in education, Criativo was incubated within Aditya and stress-tested across 60,000+ students and 30+ schools. This extensive real-world validation ensures that Criativo is not just another edtech solution—it is a proven system designed by educators, for educators.

After successfully transforming learning outcomes and institutional efficiency within our own ecosystem, Criativo is now ready to extend its impact to educational institutions worldwide. What began as an internal solution to enhance academic performance, optimize school operations, and improve engagement has evolved into a scalable, future-ready platform for schools, universities, and learning centers looking to embrace the next generation of education technology.

  • Connected Learning Ecosystem – Integrating institutions, educators, and learners into a unified digital framework
  • Scalable Institutional Solutions – Designed for K-12 schools, universities, and training academies
  • Enhanced Student Outcomes – Data-driven insights that improve engagement, retention, and performance
  • Global Standards, Local Relevance – Built on real-world educational experience, designed for institutions worldwide

Criativo is bridging the gap between traditional education and the future of learning, empowering institutions, educators, and students with solutions that are tested, trusted, and ready to scale.

Abhyas

Abhyas – The Learning Enhancement System (LES)

Abhyas is not just another LMS. It is a Learning Enhancement System (LES) designed to enhance classroom instruction and reinforce student learning through structured assessments, adaptive pathways, and validated testing methodologies.

Built to complement teacher-led instruction, Abhyas ensures that learning modules align with the curriculum and evolve based on educator-driven insights. The Assess – Practice – Test cycle serves as a continuous feedback loop, helping students reinforce concepts, track progress, and achieve mastery while enabling educators to fine-tune instruction.

Key Features:
  • Classroom Augmentation – Tools that enhance teacher-led instruction with real-time insights Teacher-Driven Pathways – Learning modules that align with the curriculum and adapt based on instructor feedback
  • Validated Assessments – The Assess – Practice – Test cycle reinforces knowledge retention and concept mastery
  • Minimal Time, Maximum Impact – Requires only 30-40 minutes per day for measurable learning improvements
  • Competitive Exam Readiness – Designed for K-12, JEE, NEET, and global standardized testing preparation
  • Seamless School Integration – Customizable and curriculum-aligned for educational institutions and coaching centers

Abhyas ensures that teachers remain at the center of learning, using structured methodologies to enhance classroom effectiveness and optimize student outcomes.

DigiVal

DigiVal – Digital Valuation and Exam Grading

DigiVal is the next-generation digital valuation system, transforming traditional paper based grading into a fast, accurate, and fully digital process.

Paper Based grading is slow, inconsistent, and resource-intensive. DigiVal digitizes and automates the evaluation process, eliminating administrative burdens while ensuring precision, transparency, and security.

Key Features:
  • Efficient Digital Grading – Eliminates manual paper handling and reduces grading time significantly
  • Instant, Transparent Results – Fast-tracked evaluation process with real-time access to results
  • Bias-Free, Anonymous Marking – Ensures fair and standardized assessment across all subjects
  • Secure Digital Records – Prevents loss of answer sheets with cloud-based storage and access
  • Seamless Integration – Compatible with existing school management platforms

DigiVal modernizes the grading process, bringing speed, accuracy, and reliability to academic assessments.
